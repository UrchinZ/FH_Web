About Us

The RPI formula hybrid team prepares students to use their skills and knowledge to succeed in an engineering workplace, practice professionalism, and demonstrate skill in project management techniques. The team strives to design, build, and enter a racecar in the Formula Hybrid Competition. The competition teaches through means of application; students take knowledge learned in the classroom and apply it to the vehicle. In addition, the team provides a focused, student-run, and innovative environment. Students learn to work with dedicated, self-driven members from diverse disciplinary backgrounds.

About Us

The RPI formula hybrid team prepares students to use their skills and knowledge to succeed in an engineering workplace, practice professionalism, and demonstrate skill in project management techniques.

Vision

The team strives to design, build, and enter a racecar in the Formula Hybrid Competition. The competition teaches through means of application; students take knowledge learned in the classroom and apply it to the vehicle. In addition, the team provides a focused, student-run, and innovative environment. Students learn to work with dedicated, self-driven members from diverse disciplinary backgrounds.

Learn

  • Project Skills: Learn application of technical, practical, managerial, and organizational skills into developing a project/product.
  • Professional Engineering Practices: Learn practices and standards common in industry for building career potential.
  • Innovation: Learn to carry new concepts through conception, via research, design, iterative engineering, and experimentation.

Compete

    Competition Goals for the Vehicle:

  • Safe: Measured by passing competition safety inspection.
  • High Performance: Measured through execution in dynamic events.
  • Ecologically Responsible: Manufacturing the car has limited environmental impact and balances performance with efficiency.
